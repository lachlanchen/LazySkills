# Musia Best Am I Mixed Route

`Best Am I · 我是天下第一等` is the canonical compact mixed EN/ZH/JP ACE route.

Core memory:

```text
Dense mixed lyric failed.
Compact XL Turbo worked.
XL SFT hallucinated generic outro text.
Website lyric was corrected from selected audio, not the draft prompt.
Fresh song-specific megastructure cover worked very well.
```

Reusable route:

1. Write native public lyric for intent.
2. Rewrite into compact ACE-facing performance lyric with one language phrase per
   line and repeated hook.
3. Use English lead plus short Mandarin pinyin and Japanese romaji only as the
   active mixed vocal sound-control layer.
4. Run XL Turbo seed sweep first; test SFT only as comparison.
5. Select by vocal beauty, loudness, ASR coverage, and hook recovery.
6. Build correction packet from full mix and vocal stem with medium/large-v3 and
   no-VAD.
7. Publish the actual sung compact structure; omit unsung draft lines.
8. Generate a fresh 16:9 cover from the song theme, ideally cinematic
   megastructure scale with one warm human focal point, no text/logo/watermark.

Correction examples:

- Restore `Best am I` over ASR guesses like `Best of my`.
- Restore `Wo shi tian xia di yi deng` over `Washi tian shadi den`.
- Restore `Dare ni mo mienai namida` when ASR produces approximate romaji.
- Omit unsung bridge/final lines instead of forcing them into subtitles.
