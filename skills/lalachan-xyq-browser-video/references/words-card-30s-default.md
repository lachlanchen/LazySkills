# Words Card and 30s Default

Use this when preparing LALACHAN Xiaoyunque prompts with the eight-image
reference set.

## Duration

Default target: `30秒`.

Do not silently compress a story to `15秒` just because the `沉浸式短片` controls
are capped at 15 seconds. Use the 30s-capable Agent/integrated workflow instead.

Use `15秒` only when the user explicitly asks for `15s`, quick test, cheapest /
least credits, or accepts the short-film cap.

## Words Card Prompt

Use `words-card.jpg` as `图1` unless a fresh generated card already exists. Treat
it as a small physical prop inside the scene:

```text
图1 是小白屏学习卡风格参考，可作为场景边缘、桌面、道具架或实验台上的小道具。
卡片内容是 English: WORD；Japanese: 日本語；Furigana: ふりがな；中文：中文含义。
它只是场景里的真实道具，不是字幕，也不是画面说明文字。
```

Pick the word from the story theme. Example:

```text
English: courage
Japanese: 勇気
Furigana: ゆうき
中文：勇气
```

The card should support the scene, not replace the story or become overlay text.
